# AVIVO Pricing & Plans

## Starter Plan — $49/month
- Up to 100 contracts per month
- Standard NLP extraction (parties, dates, amounts)
- Email support
- 5 GB document storage
- 1 user seat

## Professional Plan — $199/month
- Up to 1,000 contracts per month
- Advanced AI extraction (clauses, risks, obligations, penalties)
- Priority email + chat support
- 50 GB document storage
- Up to 10 user seats
- API access (10,000 calls/month)
- Dashboard analytics

## Enterprise Plan — Custom Pricing
- Unlimited contracts
- Full AI suite including custom model fine-tuning
- Dedicated account manager
- Unlimited storage
- Unlimited user seats
- SLA guarantee (99.9% uptime)
- On-premise deployment option
- SSO (SAML 2.0) integration

## Free Trial

All plans come with a 14-day free trial. No credit card required. You can upgrade, downgrade, or cancel at any time. Annual billing offers a 20% discount compared to monthly billing.

## Education & Non-Profit Discount

Educational institutions and registered non-profits are eligible for a 40% discount on all plans. Contact sales@avivo.com with proof of status to apply.
