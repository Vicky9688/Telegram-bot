# AVIVO Company Policy — Refunds & Returns

## Refund Policy

Customers are eligible for a full refund within 30 days of purchase if the product is returned in its original condition. After 30 days, refunds are not available, but store credit may be issued at the manager's discretion.

Digital products and software licenses are non-refundable once activated or downloaded. Exceptions may be made for technical issues that cannot be resolved by the support team within 5 business days.

## Return Process

To initiate a return, customers must:
1. Contact support at support@avivo.com with their order number.
2. Receive a Return Merchandise Authorization (RMA) code.
3. Ship the item back with the RMA code clearly labeled on the package.

Return shipping costs are the responsibility of the customer unless the return is due to a defect or error on AVIVO's part.

## Exchange Policy

Exchanges are accepted within 14 days of purchase. The replacement product will be shipped after the original item is received and inspected. Exchanges are subject to product availability.

## Damaged or Defective Items

If you receive a damaged or defective item, please contact us within 7 days of delivery with photos of the damage. We will send a replacement at no charge or issue a full refund, based on your preference.
