# AVIVO Platform — New User Onboarding Guide

## Getting Started

Welcome to AVIVO Contract Intelligence! This guide will help you get up and running in under 15 minutes.

## Step 1 — Create Your Account

Visit app.avivo.com and click "Sign Up". Enter your business email and create a password. You will receive a verification email within 2 minutes. Click the verification link to activate your account.

If you are joining an existing organization, ask your admin to send you an invitation link instead. This will automatically connect your account to your organization's workspace.

## Step 2 — Upload Your First Contract

Once logged in, click the "Upload" button on the dashboard. Drag and drop a PDF or DOCX file (maximum 50MB per file). The AI will begin processing it immediately. Processing typically takes 10–30 seconds for a standard contract.

## Step 3 — Review Extracted Data

After processing, the platform displays all extracted information including parties, effective dates, expiry dates, payment terms, and key clauses. Each extracted field shows a confidence score. Fields with low confidence (below 80%) are highlighted in yellow for manual review.

## Step 4 — Set Up Alerts

Navigate to Settings → Alerts to configure deadline reminders. You can set alerts for contract expiry, renewal windows, payment milestones, and custom date fields. Alerts can be sent via email, SMS, or Slack integration.

## Step 5 — Invite Your Team

Go to Settings → Team Members and click "Invite". Enter your colleague's email address and assign them a role: Viewer (read-only), Editor (can annotate), or Admin (full access). Team members receive an invitation email with a one-click join link.

## Keyboard Shortcuts

- `U` — Upload a new contract
- `S` — Open search
- `F` — Toggle filters
- `Ctrl + K` — Command palette (quick actions)
- `?` — Show all shortcuts
