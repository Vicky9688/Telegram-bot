# Tech FAQ — AVIVO Contract Intelligence Platform

## What is Contract Intelligence?

Contract Intelligence is AVIVO's AI-powered platform that automates the extraction, analysis, and management of contracts. It uses natural language processing to identify key clauses, obligations, risks, and deadlines across large volumes of contracts.

## How does the AI extract contract data?

The platform uses a combination of transformer-based NLP models fine-tuned on legal text and rule-based extraction pipelines. Key entities like parties, dates, payment terms, termination clauses, and penalties are automatically identified and structured into a searchable database.

## What file formats are supported?

The platform supports PDF, DOCX, TXT, and scanned documents (via OCR). Batch upload is available for processing large contract repositories at once.

## Is my data secure?

Yes. All documents are encrypted at rest using AES-256 and in transit using TLS 1.3. AVIVO is SOC 2 Type II certified. Customer data is never used for model training without explicit consent.

## Can I integrate with existing systems?

The platform offers REST API integration with popular tools including Salesforce, SAP, Microsoft Dynamics, and SharePoint. Webhooks are also supported for custom workflow automation.

## What is the typical processing time?

A standard contract (10–20 pages) is processed in under 30 seconds. Batch jobs of 1000+ contracts typically complete within 2 hours, depending on document complexity.

## How do I reset my password?

Go to the login page, click "Forgot Password", and enter your registered email. A reset link will be sent within 2 minutes. If you don't receive it, check your spam folder or contact support.
